9 Storage & Security of Your Information

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

11 Your Rights Under POPIA

As a data subject under the Protection of Personal Information Act (Act 4 of 2013), you have the following rights with respect to your personal information held by us:

  • Right of Access: You may request access to the personal information we hold about you, including the purpose for which it is being processed.
  • Right to Rectification: If you believe any of your personal information is inaccurate or incomplete, you may request that we correct or update it.
  • Right to Erasure (Deletion): You may request the deletion of your personal information, provided it is not required for legal, regulatory, or contractual purposes.
  • Right to Object: You may object to the processing of your personal information if you believe it is being used in a way that is inconsistent with your rights or the provisions of POPIA.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where your information is being processed on the basis of your consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time ‚Äî without affecting the lawfulness of processing that took place before the withdrawal.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: If you are unsatisfied with how we have handled your personal information, you may lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa.
