Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2024

1. Introduction

ProShield Pest Control ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our pest control services.

We specialize in professional pest elimination services including fumigation, bed bug treatment, rodent control, and insect extermination. Your privacy is important to us, especially when we handle sensitive information about your property and pest control needs.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information: When you request our services, we collect:

  • Full name and contact details (email, phone number)
  • Property address and specific location details for treatment
  • Service preferences and pest control history
  • Payment information for service transactions

Property Information: As a pest control service, we may document:

  • Property size and layout details relevant to treatment
  • Specific pest issues and infestation locations
  • Photos of affected areas (with your permission)
  • Treatment plans and chemical application records

Technical Data: We automatically collect website usage data through cookies and similar technologies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Scheduling and providing pest control services
  • Creating treatment plans specific to your property
  • Communicating service details, follow-ups, and safety instructions
  • Processing payments and sending invoices
  • Maintaining treatment records for warranty purposes
  • Sending service reminders and maintenance schedules
  • Improving our services and website experience
  • Complying with legal obligations in pest control industry

4. Pest Control Specific Data Handling

As pest control professionals, we handle sensitive location data:

  • Property Access Information: We securely store access details only for scheduled treatments
  • Treatment Records: We maintain detailed records of chemicals used, application areas, and safety measures
  • Safety Data: Information about pets, children, or sensitive individuals at the property
  • Follow-up Requirements: Data about required follow-up treatments or inspections

All treatment records are kept confidential and shared only with authorized personnel involved in your service.

5. Data Security for Service Operations

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted storage of client information and treatment records
  • Secure transmission of service requests and documents
  • Limited access to property information among technicians
  • Regular security audits of our data systems
  • Secure disposal of physical treatment records

Our technicians are trained in data privacy as part of their professional certification.

6. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Third parties who assist in our operations (scheduling software, payment processors)
  • Regulatory Authorities: When required by law for pest control compliance
  • Emergency Services: If health or safety concerns arise during treatment
  • Property Managers: Only with explicit consent for rental properties

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information and treatment records
  • Correct inaccurate information about your property or services
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your treatment history
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide ongoing pest control services
  • Maintain treatment warranties (typically 3-5 years)
  • Comply with pest control industry regulations
  • Resolve disputes or service issues

Treatment records are typically retained for 5 years for warranty and safety purposes.
